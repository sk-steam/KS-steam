Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I download games?

A: Go to Settings → Download for PC and choose your preferred version.

Q: How do I install modifications?

A: Go to Settings → Modifications and click Install on any mod.

Q: Can I change the language?

A: Yes! Go to Settings → Appearance and select your language.

Q: Is this launcher compatible with the browser?

A: Yes! KS Steam works in both launcher and browser modes.
